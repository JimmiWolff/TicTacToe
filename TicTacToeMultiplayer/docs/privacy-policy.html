Privacy Policy for The Wolff - Tic Tac Toe

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Summary

We collect only what's necessary for multiplayer gameplay. Your username and game statistics are stored to enable online play and leaderboards. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Overview

The Wolff - Tic Tac Toe ("the App") is a real-time multiplayer game for iOS devices. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

To provide multiplayer functionality, we collect and store:

  • Authentication Data: Email address and user ID from your Auth0 login (Google, Apple, or email)
  • Username: Your chosen display name for the game
  • Game Statistics: Wins, losses, draws, and win rate
  • Game History: Active game rooms you're participating in

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your game session
  • Display your username to other players during games
  • Track and display your game statistics
  • Show leaderboard rankings
  • Enable you to rejoin active games

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on:

  • Auth0: Authentication credentials (industry-standard secure authentication)
  • MongoDB Atlas: Game statistics and user profiles (encrypted, cloud-hosted database)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Other Players: Your username is visible to opponents during games and on the public leaderboard
  • Service Providers: Auth0 for authentication, MongoDB Atlas for data storage

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Auth0: For secure user authentication (Auth0 Privacy Policy)
  • MongoDB Atlas: For database storage (MongoDB Privacy Policy)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact:

  • Email: tictactoe.wolff@gmail.com

Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
