Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?

Tap "Quick Play" to instantly join a game with a random opponent, or tap "Create New Game" to get a room code you can share with friends.

How do I play with a friend?

Tap "Create New Game" to get a unique room code. Share this code with your friend, and they can join using "Join with Code".

What is the movement phase?

After each player places 3 pieces, the game enters the movement phase. Instead of placing new pieces, you move your existing pieces to empty squares to get three in a row.

How do I view my statistics?

Tap "View Highscores" on the main screen to see your personal stats (wins, losses, draws, win rate) and the global leaderboard.

Is my data collected?

We only store your username, email (for authentication), and game statistics. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
